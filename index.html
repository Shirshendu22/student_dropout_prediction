AI Dropout Prediction & Counseling System

How to Use This Website

  • Use the Students Academic Zone to view academic performance and simulate dropout predictions.
  • Use the Attendance Zone to monitor attendance and assess dropout risk.
  • Click Counsel to auto-generate messages for guardians based on student risk level.
  • Use filters to view specific years or switch between prediction models.

About this System

This AI-assisted Dropout Prediction System was built by the Institute of Management and Technology RSM to help students and staff proactively identify academic risks.

It analyzes attendance, internal marks, assignment completion, fee status, and last semester performance to compute a dropout risk score.

Teachers can send counseling messages to guardians, see visualizations, and simulate ML predictions on the client side.

This demo is for educational purposes and can be extended to use real ML backends and SMS gateways.
